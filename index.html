Unlocking Circular Potential

Turning Industrial Waste into Opportunity for a Greener Sarawak

Executive Summary

Malaysia currently prohibits aluminium slag reuse due to its classification as hazardous (SW 104). This proposal calls for a feasibility study and regulatory reform for both steel slag and aluminium slag, using Taiwan as a benchmark for circular solutions.
