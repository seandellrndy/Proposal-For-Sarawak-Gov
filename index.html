Executive Summary

Malaysia currently classifies aluminium slag under Scheduled Waste (SW 104) and lacks a clear reuse pathway for steel slag, resulting in landfill accumulation. This proposal recommends a feasibility study and regulatory shift, inspired by Taiwan's proven model of circular industrial reuse.

Current Challenge

Industrial slag waste accumulating in landfills without structured reuse policies

Proposed Solution

Comprehensive feasibility study and policy framework for slag valorisation

Inspiration Model

Taiwan's successful circular economy approach to industrial byproduct reuse
